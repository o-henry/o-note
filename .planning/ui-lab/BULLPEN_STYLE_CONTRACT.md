# Bullpen Style Contract

o-note's active visual target is the Bullpen operational shell.

## Source Observations

- Shell: fixed 17rem left sidebar with a content inset, no floating page card.
- Geometry: radius is zero across app surfaces, menus, buttons, and inputs.
- Hierarchy: borders and whitespace carry structure; shadows and glow are absent.
- Density: sidebar rows are compact and list-like, with mono uppercase section labels and counters.
- Content: report pages use a top status strip, large readable title area, underline tabs, and a right source/status rail.
- Controls: buttons are rectangular, text-first, and quiet until hover or active state.
- Tone: dark neutral background, restrained contrast, no saturated gradient or decorative background.

## o-note Application Rules

- Keep note creation, search, import/export, backup, reliability, and render tabs visible without marketing copy.
- HTML previews stay sandboxed and framed as a working artifact surface, not a decorative preview card.
- Markdown/source editing surfaces use monospace and stable dimensions to prevent layout shift.
- Search, note rows, and health rows should scan like an ops console: compact title, format/meta, and a clear active state.
- Browser verification is required for any claim of visual completion.
